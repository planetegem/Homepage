"Het Vleeskanon van Clementijn" is a Belgian sci-fi novel currently being written by famed author, Niels Van Damme.

Its title can be translated to "Clementine's Meatcannon", a meatcannon being a typical Belgian term to indicate a person's bank account. The story takes place in a far future where Belgium has become an intergalactic colonial power.

Proficiency in Dutch is required.
